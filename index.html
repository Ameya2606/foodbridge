About Us

We Believe No Good Food Should Go to Waste

FoodBridge is a non-profit organization dedicated to reducing food waste and fighting hunger in our communities. Founded in 2021, we have grown to serve 15 cities across India with a network of dedicated volunteers.

We partner with hotels, restaurants, wedding venues, and supermarkets to rescue surplus food before it is discarded — and redistribute it with care.

Our Mission

Reduce food waste at the source by building donor partnerships

Ensure safe, nutritious meals reach those who need them daily

Educate communities on sustainable consumption habits

Build a volunteer network that cares and acts locally
