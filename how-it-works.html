How It Works

From your surplus to someone's plate — in just three simple steps.

The Process

Simple. Fast. Impactful.

Here's exactly what happens when you donate food through FoodBridge.

🍱
Step 01

You Donate

Register your surplus food using our website contact form or call us directly. Tell us what food you have, the quantity, and your pickup address. Donations are accepted from restaurants, households, wedding venues, canteens, and more.

📞
Step 02

We Confirm

Our team reviews your request and assigns a nearby volunteer. You receive a confirmation with the volunteer's name and estimated pickup time — usually within 1–2 hours of your request.

🚗
Step 03

Volunteer Collects

A trained volunteer arrives at your location, inspects food quality, and packs it safely. All volunteers follow hygiene guidelines to ensure food remains safe for consumption during transport.

🤝
Step 04

Food is Distributed

The food is delivered to our partner shelters, orphanages, old age homes, or community kitchens. We ensure it reaches people in need the same day to maintain freshness and nutrition.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of food do you accept? ▼
We accept cooked meals, packaged food, fruits, vegetables, bread, and dairy — as long as it is fresh and safe to eat. We do not accept expired, spoiled, or contaminated food.
How quickly will someone pick up the food? ▼
We aim to send a volunteer within 1–2 hours of your confirmed request, depending on volunteer availability in your area.
Is there any cost to donate? ▼
No. Donating food through FoodBridge is completely free. We cover all logistics and volunteer coordination at no charge to the donor.
Can I become a volunteer? ▼
Yes! Fill out our contact form or visit the Donate page to register as a volunteer. We welcome anyone who can spare a few hours per week.
