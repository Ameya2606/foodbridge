About FoodBridge

We believe no good food should go to waste while people go hungry.

Who We Are

Our Mission & Vision

Bridging the gap between surplus and need.

🎯

Our Mission

To rescue surplus food from restaurants, events, and households and redistribute it to underprivileged communities — reducing waste and fighting hunger at the same time.

🌍

Our Vision

A world where no nutritious food is wasted, every community has enough to eat, and every citizen plays a role in building a sustainable food system.

Our Story

Started with One Meal

FoodBridge was founded in 2021 by a group of students from Pune who noticed massive food waste at college events while families nearby went hungry. What began as a small weekend initiative has grown into a full NGO operating across 15 cities.

Today, we partner with over 80 restaurants, hotels, and event venues to rescue food daily. Our volunteer network of 320+ people ensures every meal reaches a shelter, orphanage, or community kitchen within hours of collection.
